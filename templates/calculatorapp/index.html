About

Our Calculator app can perform mathematical operations and not only

Instructions

  • Pi (π) — pi ≈ 3,14
  • E (e) — exponential constant e ≈ 2,718
  • Degree (°) — conversion factor from radians to degrees
  • Infinity or inf — a symbol that represents a positive infinite quantity.
  • n! — gives the factorial of n.
  • Derivative (') — symbolic and numerical derivatives for arguments and parameters
  • I (i) — root of -1
  • Gamma[z] — the Euler gamma function
  • Beta[a,b] — gives the Euler beta function .
  • BesselJ[n,z] — gives the Bessel function of the first kind
  • DifferentialRoot[lde][x] — gives the holonomic function , specified by the linear differential equation lde[h,x].
  • Re[z] gives the real part of the complex number z.
  • Im[z] gives the imaginary part of the complex number z.
  • Abs — absolute value
  • Arg — argument (phase angle in radians)
  • Sign — normalized direction ()
  • Plus (+) — addition of numbers, lists, arrays, or symbolic expressions
  • Subtract (-) — subtraction
  • Times (*) — multiplication
  • Divide (/) — division
  • Power (^ or **) — power
  • Sqrt — square root
  • % — Mod
  • ( ) — Parentheses
  • Min, Max — minimum, maximum of numbers or lists
  • RealAbs — absolute value of real numbers
  • Abs — absolute value of complex numbers
  • Less (<)
  • Greater (>)
  • LessEqual (<=)
  • GreaterEqual (>=)
  • Log — base-e logarithm (Log[b,x] uses base b)
  • Log10, Log2 — base-10, base-2 logarithms
  • Exp — exponential
  • Sin ▪ Cos ▪ Tan ▪ ArcSin ▪ ArcCos ▪ ArcTan — Trigonometric Functions
  • Sinh ▪ Cosh ▪ Tanh ▪ ArcSinh ▪ ArcCosh ▪ ArcTanh — Hyperbolic Functions
  • Limit[f, x to x*] — gives the limit of f(x).
  • Integrate (a to b)(f(x), x) — gives the definite integral
  • Integrate(f(x), x) — gives the indefinite integral
  • f(x)' or derivate(f(x)) — represents the derivative of a function f of one argument.
